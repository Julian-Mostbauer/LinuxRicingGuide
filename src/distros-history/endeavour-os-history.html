EndeavourOS History

EndeavourOS is a rolling-release Linux distribution based on Arch Linux. It comes with a friendly community and a helpful forum. The distribution is aimed at people who want a fast, stable, and clean system.

Year-by-Year Summary of EndeavourOS's History

  • 2019 – The Beginning

    EndeavourOS was first released in July 2019, providing a user-friendly Arch-based distribution with a focus on simplicity and community support.

  • 2020 – EndeavourOS 2020.04.11

    EndeavourOS 2020.04.11 was released in April 2020, featuring various improvements to the system's performance and user experience.

  • 2021 – EndeavourOS 2021.02.03

    EndeavourOS 2021.02.03 was released in February 2021, introducing new features and enhancements to the system's stability and usability.

  • 2022 – EndeavourOS 2022.06.01

    EndeavourOS 2022.06.01 was released in June 2022, providing numerous improvements to the system's performance, stability, and user interface.

  • 2023 – EndeavourOS 2023.03.01

    EndeavourOS 2023.03.01 was released in March 2023, featuring various updates to the system's software packages and overall performance.
