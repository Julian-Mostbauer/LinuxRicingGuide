What is Linux Ricing?

Ricing is similar to the modding or tuning community, but for your computer. It involves customizing your desktop to make it look more visually appealing by changing themes, icons, fonts, and other visual elements.

General Overview

  • Window-Manager/Desktop-Environment: Users may prefer lightweight options like i3 or Openbox or complex ones like GNOME or KDE Plasma.
  • Wallpaper: Choose from various wallpapers to personalize your desktop.
  • Icons: Replace desktop icons to match your system's aesthetic.
  • Fonts: Select fonts like Nerd-Fonts or Powerline to enhance your system's look.
  • Themes:
    • GTK or QT Themes for colors and widgets.
    • Icon packs for system-wide icons.
    • Terminal themes for colors and fonts.
