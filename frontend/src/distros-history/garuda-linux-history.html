Garuda Linux History

Garuda Linux is a rolling-release Linux distribution based on Arch Linux. It is designed to provide a user-friendly and visually appealing experience with a focus on performance and gaming.

  • 2020 – The Beginning

    Garuda Linux was first released in 2020, offering a range of desktop environments and a focus on performance and gaming.

  • 2021 – Garuda Linux 2021.03.01

    Garuda Linux 2021.03.01 was released in March 2021, featuring various improvements to the system's performance and user experience.

  • 2022 – Garuda Linux 2022.06.01

    Garuda Linux 2022.06.01 was released in June 2022, providing numerous updates to the system's software packages and overall performance.

  • 2023 – Garuda Linux 2023.03.01

    Garuda Linux 2023.03.01 was released in March 2023, featuring various enhancements to the system's stability, performance, and gaming capabilities.
